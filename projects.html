During Engineering and Inventional Lab, an evening class at my school, I worked with a team to create a sustainable and cost-friendly option for teaching music to children. Schools often face the problem of low music department funding, and as a result, are unable to expose students to certain instruments.

Our solution? Muzoid!

Muzoid provides cost-friendly and sustainable music access and targets students.

What did we do? We built and coded a prototype, created marketing ads based on surveys sent to our local communities needs, and pitched our idea to the head of our school's technology department.
